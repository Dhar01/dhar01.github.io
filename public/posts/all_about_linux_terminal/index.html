Let's learn: Terminal Shortcuts!

July 23, 2022 · Loknath Dhar
  • To enter virtual consoles: Ctrl+Alt+F1 to Ctrl+Alt+F6
  • Switch from one to another console: Alt+F1 to Alt+F6
  • To return to the graphical desktop: Alt+F7
commandmeaning
Ctrl+abeginning of the line
Ctrl+ckill process/stop running
Ctrl+dending terminal session
Ctrl+eend of line
Ctrl+kcut from cursor to the end of line
Ctrl+lclear screen
Ctrl+ucut from cursor to the beginning of line
Ctrl+werase word from cursor to begin
Ctrl+yget back from last cut text
Ctrl+zfroze the program/pause
Alt+bbackward one word
Alt+dcut from cursor to end of word
Alt+fforward one word
Alt+backspacecut from cursor to beginning of the word
Ctrl+shift+ccopy from terminal
Ctrl+shift+vpaste on terminal
